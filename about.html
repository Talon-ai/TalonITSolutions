Our Story

Talon IT & Forex Solutions is a youth-led business focused on bringing accessible technology and financial empowerment to the people of Mamelodi and beyond. We work at the intersection of Information Technology and Financial Markets, offering both connectivity and opportunity.

Meet the Founder

A young entrepreneur studying Software Engineering, passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems. Through Talon IT & Forex Solutions, I aim to help the youth grow, learn, and reach their full potential in both tech and finance.

Our Vision

To become a leading provider of digital infrastructure and financial education across underserved communities, empowering the next generation with tools to succeed in all areas of life.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering high-quality services in both Forex and IT, with a focus on reliability, education, and youth empowerment.
